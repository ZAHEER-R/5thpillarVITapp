About Us

Club Introduction

Our Mission

5th Pillar VIT is a socio-political NGO at VIT, dedicated to strengthening democracy through education, awareness, and civic engagement.

Our Vision

To cultivate an environment where individuals can challenge norms, express ideas, and build a transparent, democratic society.

Our Goals

Educate, promote civic engagement, and address societal issues for an informed and equitable society.

Our Motto

Encourage, enable, and empower every citizen to eliminate corruption at all levels.
