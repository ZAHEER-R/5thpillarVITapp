Committee Activities

Discover the ongoing projects and initiatives of each committee

Events Department

7 COLLABORATIONS, 25+ ARTICLES. Topped number of events chart (among all clubs and chapters) with 26 events till July, 2023. Conducted 75 events through the tenure 2023-24. Total events: 75, Riviera 2023: 5, Gravitas 2023: 1, Collaborative events: 4. Conducted informative sessions, group discussions, quizzes, hackathons, ideathons, etc., and updated all reports on time. Wide range of topics included stigmatized areas requiring awareness; some events had national-level external participants. Specialized events: Campaign Against Substance Abuse; Alumni Event; Sustainable Development Goals; Outreach Event. “Law and You” series was started to raise legal awareness.

Editorial Department

Migration of Fifth Pillar’s Blog from WordPress to Medium (preserving 200+ articles). Published 25+ new articles. Instituted a yearly Newsletter. Published reviewed works from hackathons and ideathons. Link: [https://medium.com/@5thpillar](https://medium.com/@5thpillar)

Technical Department

Integrates the club’s technical stack, manages websites and registrations, and maintains apps. Focuses on security and user-friendly experiences across web and apps. Feedback and improvement requests can be sent via the feedback page.

Design Department

Designs posts, photos, and videos, and manages social media content (Instagram, Facebook, LinkedIn). Contributes to yearly newsletters and articles publishing. Follow for updates to join upcoming initiatives.

Finance Department

Plans events, hackathons, and projects within budget, and welcomes collaborators and partners to drive impact.

Outreach Department

Markets club initiatives and events, hackathons, and articles to VIT Vellore students and beyond.
