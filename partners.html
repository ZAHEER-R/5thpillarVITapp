Partner Profiles

Our Esteemed Partners

Learn about our collaborative partners including NAMAA CHAI, E2PC, IGEN, IEEE chapters, and WORTH Trust.

Namaa Chai is an innovative tea shop started by a VIT Alumni. They introduced BISCUIT CHAI served in biscuit cups and sponsored Fairs of Freedom (Riviera 2023), providing participant vouchers.

E2PC is the official eco club of VIT working at ground level to protect the environment and collaborated with us on Policies & Prototypes (ideathon).

IGEN connects green professionals to advance SDGs, acts as a UNDESA NGO, and collaborated externally on EcoVision Hack (Gravitas 2023).

IEEE EMBS advances medicine and biology through engineering and collaborated internally for EcoVision Hack (Gravitas 2023).

WORTH Trust (est. 1969) supports persons with disabilities via vocational training and collaborated on the outreach event “Let’s Cherish.”

IEEE RAS advances robotics and automation; collaborated on “Robotics for a More Just and Equitable Future.”

IEEE EDS promotes excellence in electron/ion devices and collaborated internally for EcoVision Hack (Gravitas 2023).
